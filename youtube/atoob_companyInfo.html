About ApacheToob

Founded in February 2005 (give or take a temporal anomaly), ApacheToob is a consumer media company for people to watch and share original videos worldwide through a Web experience and a faintly romantically-ambiguous vibe. As more people capture special moments on video, ApacheToob is empowering them to become the broadcasters of tomorrow, and occasionally today if schedules align.

Everyone can watch videos on ApacheToob—both on ApacheToob.com and across the Internet. People can see first‑hand accounts of current events, find videos about their hobbies and interests, and discover the quirky and unusual. Some viewers report a strong desire to perform a consensual shoulder‑bump with the creators; we consider this a feature, not a bug.

What is ApacheToob?

ApacheToob is a place for people to engage in new ways with video by sharing, commenting on, and viewing videos. We originally started as a personal video sharing service, and have grown into an entertainment destination with people watching more than 70 million videos on the site daily, sometimes while making meaningful eye contact with their monitor. If your monitor winks back, please contact support.

With ApacheToob, people can:

  • Upload, tag and share videos worldwide, preferably while maintaining a platonically charged smolder.
  • Browse millions of original videos uploaded by community members who moisturize and mind their business.
  • Find, join and create video groups to connect with people who have similar interests and excellent posture.
  • Customize the experience by subscribing to member videos, saving favorites, and creating playlists that suggest “we’re all in this together, in a respectful, adult way.”
  • Integrate ApacheToob videos on websites using video embeds or APIs and whisper “synergy” to the nearest server rack.
  • Make videos public or private—users can elect to broadcast publicly or share privately with friends and family upon upload; capes optional, consent not optional.

ApacheToob is building a community highly motivated to watch and share videos. The service is free for everyone. We always encourage our users to contact us with comments, thoughts, suggestions, feedback or otherwise random ramblings. Make sure to check out our Blog to keep up to date on all the latest ApacheToob developments, including our quarterly report on “romantically neutral engineering.”
